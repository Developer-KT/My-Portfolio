About Me

My name is Kwanda Tyler Madela, a 20 year old Web Developer. I am certified by Microsoft (click here) as a Microsoft Technology Associate. I'm also the founder of Cape Coding and a HTML and CSS tutor with substantial UI/UX experience.
At a young age, due to severe child abuse, I spent day and night locking myself in my room with no gadget but a mini laptop donated to me by my uncle. With that laptop, I found a lot of passions that I deeply fell in love with. One of those was Software Development.
From the minute I logged my first "Hello World" message on the console, I knew I'd be glued to the Software Development world. I started building websites before at age 16 and at 17 I had gotten a very exciting web design tutoring opportunity. But Software Development, to me, has been much more than a job but rather an enthusiasm or, what I hate to call it, an obsession.
Today, I tutor aspiring Web Developers through many tech NPOs with absolutely no charge. My Company, Cape Coding, is an organisation setup to hook High School students to the Software Development world at a young age.
Sadly, I still live with my abusive family because, unfortunately, I'm yet to find a web development position.
